#The Rhetus Periander Butterfly

The Rhetus Periander Butterfly is awesome because of its unique vivid blue coloration.
Blue is the coolest color on earth as it reminds us of nature, water, space, and the sky,
Butterflies and their glittery beauty are symbols of change and transformation, comfort, hope, and positivity.
This is why the combination is the coolest to the eye.

According to Wikipedia, Rhetus Periander Butterfly is the commonest and most widespread butterfly,
which belongs to the Rhetus family Riodinidae.
It is found in most of Central America and South America, ranging from Mexico to Brazil and Argentina.
They are fast flying, strikingly iridescent, and have long wing tails.
Their ability to live under leaves makes it hard to notice them despite their vivid blue coloration.
